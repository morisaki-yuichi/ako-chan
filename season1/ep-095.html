Season 1 · Ep 95

Colonel Adelie

Movement. Behind a blue-grey rock at the edge of the valley.

A small penguin stepped out. It was very small — smaller than any penguin Ako-chan had seen — but the way it moved was not small at all.

It looked at all seven of them, fast and sharp. One second. Two seconds. Then it had finished.

"Colonel Adelie," it said. "Guard and instructor to Pen-san. I will explain the situation."

It did not wait for them to answer.

"I have been observing this coast for three months," it said. "I brought Pen-san to this valley to keep it away from the interference of the structure below the ice. Close enough to hear. Far enough to wait properly."

"How did you know we were coming?" Ako-chan asked.

"Narwhals," said the Colonel. "And whales. And seabirds." It looked at her directly. "The whole south has known you were coming. You have been noticed at every step."

Ako-chan thought about Riro's narwhal, and the orca in October, and the albatross at the Drake Passage.

"That is a long list," said Suke-san.

"Yes," said the Colonel. "You have traveled far.
