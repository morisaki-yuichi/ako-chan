Season 1 · Ep 58

Everyone Decides

"I want to cross the Pacific," said Ako-chan. "I want to go to where the lines meet."

She said it clearly. Not as a question.

"I will go," said Dango. He had already taken two steps toward the water.

"I will come," said Daru. "I want to know what is there."

"Of course," said Gabu. "There is nothing to think about."

Riro looked toward the south. "The sea is mine," he said. "Tell me when."

Everyone looked at Suke-san.

He was looking at the sea. His tail was curled. His back was very straight. The wind moved the fur at his ears.

"I will go," he said quietly.

He paused.

"I will go," he said again.

The second time was the same words. The same quiet voice. But it was different. The first one was for everyone. The second one was for himself.

Ako-chan did not ask why he said it twice.

"Then we are going," she said.

Obia was already making notes on the terminal.

"I will begin preparations tonight," it said.

Dango was still looking at the water. His tail was going at full speed. He had made his decision. He was happy with it.
