Season 1 · Ep 49

The End of June

They made a fire on the flat ground near the cliff. The smoke went straight up in the still air. The stars were bright.

Six of them sat around the fire. Ako-chan, Dango, Obia, Daru, Gabu, Suke-san.

Daru was telling a story about the market in China. He was on the part where he was sitting on the shelf between the stone rabbit and the clay pot.

"The light really was excellent," he said. "Very warm. Very even."

"You always say that," said Dango.

"Because it was true," said Daru.

Gabu was watching the fire. Suke-san was sitting straight with his tail curled behind him.

After a while, Suke-san got up. He walked to the edge of the firelight. There was a bundle of dried kelp near the rocks. He had put it there earlier. He picked it up in his mouth and carried it back to the fire.

He set it down in the middle of the group.

Everyone looked at it.

"For the journey," said Suke-san.

"What is it for?" said Daru.

"I am not sure," said Suke-san. "But it is useful."

Ako-chan looked at the kelp. She was not sure what they would do with dried kelp. But she picked it up and put it carefully into the pack.

"Thank you," she said.

"You are welcome," said Suke-san.

He sat back down. The fire burned.

Below the cliff, the sea moved quietly in the dark. Tomorrow they would go down to the rocks. Something was waiting for them there.
