Season 1 · Ep 94

I Have Been Waiting

The chick stood up.

It was small, but it stood straight. Its back was perfectly upright. Its flippers hung at its sides. It looked at all of them at once.

Then it spoke.

"I am Pen-san," it said. "I have been waiting for you."

Everyone was quiet.

Pen-san looked at Ako-chan. "Your name is Ako-chan."

Then at the dog beside her. "And you are Dango."

"How do you—" Ako-chan started.

Pen-san continued. It said each name in turn. Obia. Gabu. Daru. Suke-san. Riro.

All seven names. All correct.

Ako-chan looked at Dango. Dango looked at Ako-chan. She looked at Obia. Obia was very still.

"How do you know our names?" Ako-chan asked.

Pen-san blinked for the first time.

"I knew you were coming," it said. "I have known for a long time. I cannot explain it better than that."

It settled back down onto the ice, perfectly still again.

All seven of them looked at each other. Then they looked at the small round penguin chick who had been waiting in this valley for them.

Nobody said anything for a while.
