Season 1 · Ep 35

Rivals

0:00

The hill in Hokkaido was steep and open. At the top, the sky was very large. The sea was far to the east, grey and flat.

Suke-san walked to the highest point and stood there. His tail was straight up.

He looked at Obia.

"My nose is better than yours," he said.

"Possibly," said Obia.

"I'm faster too."

"Possibly."

"I'm better."

"You may be right," said Obia.

Suke-san looked at Obia. He had expected an argument. Obia had not given one. He didn't know what to do with that.

Dango had been sitting nearby, watching. Then he got up and ran — just ran, down the hill and back up, for no reason.

Suke-san watched him. Then he ran too.

They went down the hill together and came back up. Dango fast. Suke-san fast. Not racing. Not trying to beat each other. Just running at the same speed, in the same direction, side by side.

After a long time they stopped at the top. Both were breathing hard.

Dango's collar was a bright orange. Suke-san's tail was high and still.

They walked back to the others. Neither said anything.

Ako-chan watched them come. She did not say anything either.
