Obia Stops Again

They were walking along a wide path above the sea when Obia stopped.

Not a slow stop. A sudden one. Mid-step, with one foot in the air.

Five seconds passed.

Dango reacted first. He trotted back and pressed his front paw firmly against Obia's leg. "Are you okay?"

No answer.

"Obia," said Dango. "Obia."

Daru leaned forward from the top of the backpack. "Is it broken?" he asked.

"It is not broken," said Ako-chan quickly. She had seen this before.

On the path ahead, Gabu had stopped too. He turned and looked. Then, without saying anything, he walked slowly back to where Obia stood. He walked to Obia's side. He stood there.

That was all. He did not touch anything. He did not speak.

Obia came back. One blink. Then normal movement returned. It set its foot down and straightened.

"A minor adjustment," it said.

Dango stepped back. "Same as before?"

"Same as before."

Dango looked at Gabu. Gabu was already walking back to his place at the rear of the group. He had not said a word.

Later, Ako-chan asked him why he had done it.

Gabu looked at her. "It seemed like the right thing to do," he said.
