Season 1 · Ep 103

Gabu's Observation

The stairs went down for a long time. The walls were smooth stone and pale metal. The light came from long strips set into the ceiling. It was warm underground. Warmer than Ako-chan expected.

At the bottom, a wide corridor led ahead. The group stopped there for a moment.

Gabu was quiet. He walked slowly and did not speak. He was watching Obia.

Then Gabu turned his head.

"Obia," he said.

His voice was quiet. It was not angry. It was not accusing. It was careful and clear.

"This place knows you," said Gabu.

Everyone stopped.

That was all Gabu said. He did not ask a question. He did not demand an explanation. He simply said what he had seen.

The door had opened for Obia. The lights were on. The stairs were ready. Everything here had been waiting for them — waiting for Obia specifically.

Obia stood very still.

"...Yes," said Obia.

One word.

Dango looked up at Obia. Riro pressed a paw to its face. Daru opened its mouth and then closed it. Suke-san's tail was very still.

Ako-chan watched Obia. Obia was not looking away.

"Yes," Obia said again. Quieter this time. "This place knows me."
