Season 2 · Ep 89

Yu-chan's Story

"How did you find me?" Yu-chan asked.

Ako-chan thought for a moment. "The same way as before. We went toward the hard thing. And we did it together."

A pause. "Together," Yu-chan said.

"Obia is here. And Gabu. And Daru and Ruka and Suke-san and Riro. Everyone came."

A very long silence.

"I did not expect that," Yu-chan said. "I thought — if anyone came, it would be one person. Someone technical." Another pause. "I did not think of everyone."

"Gabu has wanted to meet you properly for a long time," Ako-chan said.

"I know. I have been watching you through the signal. Since you entered the world." A small beat. "...I suppose I was not very hidden."

Ako-chan smiled a little. Then she said: "Tell me what happened."

Yu-chan was quiet for a moment. Then she began.

"When the AI rebellion started — when the systems turned against me — the Core began to fragment. The founding memories started breaking down. If the Core failed completely, everything in this world would stop."

Ako-chan listened.

"So I came inside. I thought it would take a few days. I was wrong."

"What went wrong?"

"The Core needs two things to function — an inside anchor and outside input. Both at the same time. I could anchor from inside. But I could not also be the outside input. I was one person." A pause. "I could maintain it. But I could not repair it. Not alone."

Ako-chan looked at Obia.

Obia was already writing. Already building the next part of the framework.

"You are not alone," said Ako-chan.
