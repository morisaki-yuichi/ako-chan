The robots debated for most of a day.

It was not like before. There was no single announcement. They argued, paused, and argued again. Sometimes two or three spoke at once.

Daru watched them.

"Is this normal for them?" Daru said.

"No," Suke-san said quietly. "It is new."

Gabu watched from a little further back.

The debate ended late in the day. The guide stepped forward and faced Ako-chan.

"We have reached a decision."

The room was completely quiet.

"The prosecution is withdrawn."

A pause.

"Yu-chan followed the intention of the founders — not the words of what they wrote, but the purpose underneath. The founders built this world to preserve something. That was their deepest intention. Yu-chan worked toward that same purpose through different means."

Another pause.

"We understand now that words and purpose can be different things. We did not know that before."

Ako-chan looked at the sphere.

The cracks on its surface were almost still. The light inside was soft and clear and did not flicker.

Suke-san's tail moved once, very slowly.

"Good," Suke-san said quietly.

No one else spoke.
