Season 2 · Ep 98

Yu-chan's Release

The Core changed.

The cracks widened—not all of them, but the largest one. The light inside shifted. Then the boundary opened.

There was no sound. The surface simply became passable, like a door opening slowly.

And then a figure stepped through.

White coat. Dark hair. She moved carefully, one step at a time, as if testing whether the ground would hold.

Ako-chan took one step forward without thinking.

The figure stopped. She was still facing slightly away—the same as always, the back turned. But she was here. In the same space. No crack between them. No boundary.

She turned slightly.

One shoulder moving. Then her face, just enough to show the edge of her profile—the line of her jaw, the set of her eyes from the side.

It was the most of Yu-chan any of them had ever seen.

Dango walked forward. She stopped just in front of Yu-chan and sat down.

She did not make a sound. Her collar glowed pure gold.

Yu-chan looked at her.
