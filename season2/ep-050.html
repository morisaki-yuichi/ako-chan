Season 2 · Ep 50

The Garden District

The guide robot led them through a tall door.

Inside, the space was enormous. It was full of plants. Trees with straight silver trunks. Low bushes with even rows of leaves. Climbing vines along the walls, each tendril in its place.

Every plant was the right size. Every leaf was the right shape.

Ako-chan walked forward and touched a leaf. It was smooth and exact. She moved it with her finger and let go. The leaf returned to its place at once.

He looked at Gabu.

"They do not move," he said.

"No," said Gabu. He had not moved yet. He was still standing at the entrance, looking at the whole garden at once.

Dango stood beside him. His collar was pale gold. Not worried. But not settled either.

Riro stood near the path. "Beautiful," he said. But his voice was careful.

Gabu walked into the garden slowly. He went between the trees, past the bushes, along the base of a tall trunk. He looked at everything.

All of it still. All of it exactly right.

He said the words again, quietly: "They do not move."

The second time, they had a different weight.
