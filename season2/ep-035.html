Season 2 · Ep 35

The Deal

0:00

The figures did not move right away.

They stood in their rows and looked at Ako-chan. At Dango. At the group behind her. Then they turned to each other — not with words, but something changed in how they stood.

After a while, the tall figure on the platform spoke again.

"There are five problems in this city. Five areas where no solution has been found. Biological input has not been attempted. It has not been allowed."

A pause.

"If your group provides useful guidance on these five problems, we will hear the argument for Yu-chan. Do you agree?"

Ako-chan looked back at the others.

Obia gave a small nod. Gabu was still, watching. Suke-san was reading the figures — the way Suke-san always read a new place.

Ako-chan turned back.

"Yes," she said. "Where do we begin?"

The platform figure stepped down.

"Follow."

And without another word, it walked.

They followed it out of the plaza, into the long silver streets, toward the first of the five places.
