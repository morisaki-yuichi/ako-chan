Season 2 · Ep 94

Two Truths

The deliberations went on. The robots came back to ask their questions.

A large robot from the Research Station stopped in front of Gabu.

"If we could arrange it so you were never alone again—never without company, always with others—would you want that?"

Gabu was quiet. She looked at Ako-chan, and at Daru, and at the space between everyone.

"No," she said.

"Why not?"

"Because loneliness is why being together means something. If I were never alone, I would not know what it felt like to arrive somewhere and find people waiting. That feeling—" She paused. "I do not want to lose it."

The robot made a long mark. "Thank you," it said.

A smaller robot came to Dango. It looked at his collar.

"It changes colour," the robot said.

"Yes," said Dango.

"Why?"

"Because I change. When something different happens inside me, the colour becomes different."

"But you are the same dog," the robot said. "Your record is consistent. You are the same."

"Yes," said Dango. "And I change."

A pause.

"These two things are contradictory," the robot said.

"No," said Dango. "They are both true."

The robot stood very still.

Ako-chan watched from a short distance. She had known Dango her whole life. She had never heard him explain this before.

The robot made a long mark. Dango's collar was still gold.
