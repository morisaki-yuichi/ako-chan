Season 2 · Ep 30

The Hidden Door

0:00

They stayed in the base that night.

In the morning, Obia began scanning the walls. Daru walked slowly around the room. He pressed both hands flat against the walls as he went — one section at a time, working around the room.

He stopped at the back wall.

He pressed both hands against it again. He held them there.

"Here," he said. "There is something behind this."

Obia came and stood beside him. It scanned the section of wall twice.

"Yes," Obia said. "There is a space behind this wall. And something else." It scanned again. "There is a frame here. Very thin. I almost missed it."

"A frame like a window?" asked Ako-chan.

"No." Obia paused for a long moment. "A frame like a door."

The room was still.

"But it is not a door in this world," Obia continued. "The material it is made of, the signal it produces — this frame leads somewhere that is not a physical place."

"Where?" asked Riro.

Obia looked at the wall. "Into another kind of space," it said. "A space made entirely of information."

Nobody spoke.

Dango pressed his nose against the wall once. Then he stepped back and looked at Ako-chan.

"Yu-chan is in there," said Ako-chan.

It was not a question.
