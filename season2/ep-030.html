Season 2 · Ep 30

The Hidden Door

Daru had been moving through the base all day.

In the main room, he stopped. He stood in front of the far wall. He looked at it, then at the other walls, then at the far wall again.

"Obia," he said. "This wall is different."

Obia walked over. He ran his hand slowly across the surface. In the centre, there was a seam. Very thin. Almost invisible.

"A panel," he said.

Everyone came. Even Gabu came out of Yu-chan's room.

Obia found the edges and opened it.

Behind it was a frame. The height of a person. Made of light—pale, faint, and completely steady.

Inside the frame was something that was not the wall. Not darkness. A quality of waiting.

Nobody spoke for a long time.

"It is a door," said Obia at last. "Not to a room you can walk to. It is a door to a world built inside a system. A space that exists in data, not in the physical world." She looked at the light. "And Yu-chan's code is in the structure of that space. In the foundation."

The light in the frame did not flicker.

It waited.
