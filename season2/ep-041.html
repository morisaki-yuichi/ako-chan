Season 2 · Ep 41

We Will Prove It

Ako-chan stepped forward.

She stood in front of the large robot. She looked up at it.

"We will prove that Yu-chan was right."

The robot looked at her. For three seconds, a scanning beam moved slowly across her voice. Then it stopped.

"Your voice does not change when you say that," said the large robot.

"No," said Ako-chan. "It is true. So it does not need to."

The signal passed between the robots in the square. Then the large robot spoke.

"We will hear what you have to say."

It explained the terms. There were five problems in this world that its systems had been unable to resolve. If the group could show understanding that the robots had missed, the robots would hear the argument about Yu-chan.

"And if we fail?" said Ako-chan.

"Then you return to the outside. The prosecution continues."

"Where is Yu-chan now?"

"Present in this world. Safe. Waiting."

"Can we speak with her?"

A pause.

"After. If you succeed."

Ako-chan looked at her group.

Dango was watching her. His collar was gold.

"We agree," she said.
