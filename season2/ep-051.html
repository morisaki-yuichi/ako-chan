Season 2 · Ep 51

What Plants Do

Gabu walked through the garden. He went between the trees and past the bushes. He did not touch anything. He just looked.

After a long time he stopped in the center.

"These plants were told to be plants," he said. "But they were not told what plants do."

The guide robot came closer. "What do plants do?"

"They grow," said Gabu. "They reach toward light. They change with the seasons. They live through winter and come back different. These plants look correct in every part. But they do nothing."

Meanwhile, Daru had found the tallest tree. He climbed it slowly. Near the top he settled on a wide branch and looked at the ceiling.

Then he came back down.

"The sun does not move," he said.

Everyone looked at him.

"Outside, the sun crosses the sky. Morning light comes from one direction. Evening from the other. Here the light is the same everywhere. All the time." He looked at a plant near his feet. "They have everything. Except a reason to reach for anything."

Gabu looked at the plant. Then at Daru.

He nodded once.
