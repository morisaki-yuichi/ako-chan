Season 2 · Ep 17

The Cold Water

In the afternoon, Ako-chan told Riro about the signal.

They were sitting on the rocks above the water. Riro was lying on his back in a shallow pool, floating, looking at the sky. He always thought better when he was in the water.

Ako-chan told him about the SOS. About Obia's analysis. About the name Yu-chan and the word help.

Riro floated and listened and said nothing.

The winter sky was pale and high. A gull crossed it slowly.

Ako-chan waited.

After a long time, Riro said: "Antarctica."

"Yes."

"The water around Antarctica is cold," said Riro. He said it matter-of-factly, the way he said all facts about water. "Very cold. Much colder than here."

"Yes," said Ako-chan.

"That suits me," said Riro.

He did not move from the pool. He kept floating on his back, looking at the sky. But he had made his decision.

Ako-chan understood. "Thank you, Riro."

"Where are we meeting next?" said Riro.

"The Mediterranean. Two more friends."

Riro closed his eyes. "Then I will be ready in the morning."
