Season 3 · Ep 43

By Boat

On the fifth day, a small boat came in from the harbour at midmorning.

The boy was on the rocks. He watched the boat dock. A few people stepped off. He noted the boat number and the time.

Then he saw the corgi.

He recognised it immediately. The same short legs. The same ear position. The same measured pace. He had seen this corgi at a pier in Yokohama, at a park in Tokyo, on a rocky shore in Hokkaido.

The small tortoise came after it.

Behind them, a woman with glasses and a dark bag. He could not see his face clearly.

The two tortoises on the rock moved. The pale one lifted its head. The domed one took several fast steps toward the water's edge, then stopped.

The boy stood up. He moved back toward the wall, a little further from the water, and sat down again.

He watched the woman walk toward the tortoises. They moved toward his. The domed one reached his first. He crouched down and said something to it. He could not hear the words.

He opened his notebook. Then he closed it.
