Season 3 · Ep 83

Parts in the Snow

The base had three storage rooms.

The boy went through each shelf slowly. He had his list. He took Obia out of his bag and set it on the nearest surface, screen facing up.

He held each component in front of Obia's lens before deciding.

Most items were too large or the wrong type. But he found three components on the first shelf. Two more in the second room.

The Adélie penguin had followed him in. It stood beside the open bag and looked at each piece he set down. When he placed something on the reject side, the Adélie moved it slightly with one flipper and looked at it again. When he placed something on the keep side, the Adélie did not touch it.

The boy watched this for a moment. Then he kept working.

A third room at the back had a low box of small parts. He sorted through it piece by piece, holding each one to Obia. Obia flagged three as marginal. He set those to one side.

By the time he finished, he had seven of the eight missing components — all confirmed by Obia.

For the last one, there was a substitute in his toolkit. Not ideal, but possible.

He put everything in his bag and stood up. The Adélie penguin stood by the door.
