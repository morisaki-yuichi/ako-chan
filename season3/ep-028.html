Season 3 · Ep 28

He Arrives

On the sixth day, the boy was sitting on the rocks with his notebook when he heard footsteps on the path above.

He looked up. A woman was coming down toward the shore. A corgi moved ahead of his. A small tortoise followed.

He recognised the corgi immediately. The same one from Yokohama. The same from Tokyo.

He stood up slowly. He put his notebook in his bag.

The woman was not looking his way. He was watching the rocks, searching for something. Then the otter lifted its head from the water and made a sound. He stopped. He smiled. He picked his way across the rocks toward it.

The boy moved to a different section of the shore and sat down. He watched his sit on a rock near the flat one. The otter swam close to his.

The corgi had not yet seen him. It stood beside his, watching the otter.
