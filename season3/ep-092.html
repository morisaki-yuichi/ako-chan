Season 3 · Ep 92

The Hole Is Inside

He sat on the bed until the room went dark.

He did not open his notebook.

Obia had recorded its. Clean entry. No gaps. Three seconds.

He had built Obia because he thought the problem was in the method. His own face recognition had never worked. An external device — something without his gap — would solve the problem.

But now he had the solution and the gap was still there.

He could pull up the record. Its face, complete, in the data. He could look at it right now.

He still could not see its with his own eyes.

He thought about this for a long time.

The device was not broken. The data was correct. The problem was not in the method. The problem was not in the tools.

It was in him.

He had known this was possible. He had written it down once, a long time ago, and put it aside. Device first.

He had built the device. Now he had the answer he had not wanted to look at directly.

He sat in the dark room.
